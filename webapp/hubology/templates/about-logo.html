The hub-ology logo comes from small towns

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

The hub-ology logo is based on 2010 census data for towns in Spartanburg County, SC. Each circle (node) in the logo (graph) represents the population of a town in the data set. The lines (edges) connecting the circles are based on the distance (in miles) from the center circle (the city of Spartanburg). The colors for the circles come from the American Flag. Inspired by Daniel Morgan and The Battle of Cowpens, hub-ology hopes to help create many new important moments that originate in small towns.


The logo was created using the Python Programming Language and a collection of really useful modules. networkx was used to construct the graph based on the census data. matplotlib was used to plot the graph and to generate the image output. geopy was used to calculate the distances between the towns.
